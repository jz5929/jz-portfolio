This creative project is an interactive Pomodoro study simulator designed to help users stay focused and manage their time effectively. On this page, visitors can input a task they want to complete, start a 25-minute timer for concentrated work, and then take a 5 minute break when the session ends. After four cycles, the simulator prompts a longer break of 15–30 minutes.
